Our camp offers instructional swim in the morning, and recreational swim in the afternoon.

  • Instructional Swim: At this camp, there are many swim levels, and each of our highly skilled lifeguards teach a different level. Level one is for beginning swimmers and level ten is for more advanced swimmers.
  • Recreational Swim: Our afternoon recreational swim allows your children to let loose, relax, and have fun! Everyday, after recreational swim is half over, our lifeguards will blow their whistle and inform the campers to sit around the edge of the pool. The campers will sit next to the “buddy” that their counselors assigned to them. Then, the counselors make sure their campers are where they need to be. This is called a “buddy check.”
